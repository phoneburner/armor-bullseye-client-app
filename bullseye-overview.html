Bullseye

Know if your phone numbers are being flagged as spam — before your customers do.

The Problem

When carriers or call-blocking apps flag your phone numbers as "Spam Likely," your calls don't get answered. You lose revenue, miss appointments, and damage customer trust — often without knowing it's happening until the complaints roll in.

Traditional testing relies on centralized infrastructure that doesn't reflect how your numbers actually behave on your own carriers and in your own calling environment.

The Solution

Bullseye places real test calls from your own phone numbers, through your own telecom provider, and tells you exactly what happened — answered, blocked, or flagged. You get an accurate, real-world picture of your number reputation.

Real calls, real results

Test calls originate from your actual numbers on your carrier — not a third-party test line. What Bullseye sees is what your customers experience.

Fully automated

Tests run on demand or on a schedule. No manual dialing, no spreadsheets, no guesswork.

Fast to deploy

Install the lightweight agent on any machine with Docker or Python. Connect your telecom account. Start testing in minutes.

Your credentials stay with you

Telecom API keys and tokens never leave your infrastructure. Bullseye only receives the call outcome — nothing else.

Security & Privacy

Bullseye runs entirely on your infrastructure and uses your existing telecom credentials to place test calls. Your API keys, tokens, and account IDs never leave your environment. The only information sent to ARMOR® is the call status (e.g., spam label, blocked, delivered) and the phone number being tested. No call content or recordings are ever collected. All connections are encrypted with TLS and Armor Solutions is SOC 2 Type II compliant. You can remove Bullseye from your environment at any time.

Works with any provider that supports programmatic outbound voice. Additional providers can be added on request.
